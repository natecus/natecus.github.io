Wheels

We manufacture our own wheels which gives you an opportunity to choose your color, design, size, and hardness separately, it is just one more way we are trying to help you make your board YOUR board

All our wheels have a urethane core which provides great wear and also keeps the wheels lightweight. They also have a square profile to provide more even wear.


Larger wheels have greater top speed but a slower acceleration.


Harder wheels last longer while softer wheels give more grip, stability, and can make riding on rough surfaces easier.

The higher the number the softer the wheel.
